All in Good Waste: Machine Learning Trash Sorter

Along with my team of 4, we built a Raspberry Pi machine learning device with Python, Lobe.AI, and image processing that precisely and efficiently sorts through and disposes trash with 94.5% accuracy. It uses thousands of photos from the Kaggle dataset to transport metal, garbage, and recycling to their proper wastebins with a elegant system of two motors.

With the rapidly worsening state of our climate, discovering that only 9% of plastic is accurately sorted alarmed me. Our machine distinguishes between trash, paper, and plastic, transports the garbage to its correct bin, and disposes of it properly, thus creating a cleaner, more eco-friendly community while also being efficient for busy students to use.
